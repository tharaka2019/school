Frequently Asked Questions

How do I schedule a visit?

You can schedule a visit by filling out our online form above, calling us at (323) 362-4301, or emailing us at info@msdarshismlc.com. We offer tours Monday through Friday and select Saturday times. We recommend scheduling at least a week in advance to ensure availability.

What should I bring to the tour?

Please bring any questions you have about our program, your child's needs, and enrollment process. If your child will be joining the tour, we recommend comfortable clothes and closed-toe shoes. We'll provide all necessary information packets during your visit.

Can my child come with me on the tour?

Absolutely! We encourage children to visit so they can see the classroom environment and meet our teachers. This helps them feel more comfortable and excited about potentially joining our school community. However, we also offer adult-only tours if preferred.

How long does a tour typically last?

Tours typically last 45-60 minutes, including time to walk through our classrooms, meet staff, observe children (when in session), and discuss our program in detail. We also allow extra time for questions and to provide enrollment information if you're interested.

Is there parking available?

Yes, we have convenient on-site parking available for all visitors. Our location is easily accessible with clear parking spaces designated for school visitors. If you have any accessibility needs, please let us know when scheduling your visit.
