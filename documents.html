Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit completed enrollment forms?

You can submit completed forms by email to info@msdarshismlc.com, drop them off in person at our office, or mail them to our address. We recommend submitting forms at least 2 weeks before your desired start date.

What documents are required for enrollment?

Required documents include the completed enrollment application, health and medical forms with current immunization records, emergency contact information, and birth certificate. Additional forms may be required based on your child's specific needs.

How often are policies updated?

We review and update our policies annually or as needed to comply with state regulations and best practices. Parents are notified of any significant policy changes via email and updated handbooks are provided.

Can I get digital copies of all documents?

Yes, most documents are available for download as PDFs. Some documents may require a signed request for privacy reasons. Please contact our office if you need assistance accessing any specific documents.

What if I have questions about the forms?

Our staff is happy to help with any questions about forms or policies. You can call us at (323) 362-4301, email us, or schedule an appointment to review documents in person. We want to make the enrollment process as smooth as possible for families.

Need Help with Documents?

Our team is here to assist you with any questions about enrollment forms or school policies.
