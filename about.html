Meet Ms. Darshi

Ms. Darshi has dedicated her career to early childhood education, bringing over nine years of experience as a Head Teacher. Her journey in education began with a deep belief in the Montessori method and its transformative power in children's lives.

While building her career in education, Ms. Darshi also worked at Palomar College's Health Department as a Peer Mentor, helping her peers succeed in their educational goals. This experience reinforced her passion for guiding others toward their full potential.

Ms. Darshi strongly believes in Montessori methods because they allow teachers to reach students at their own level and foster independence and confidence in every child. As both the Head Teacher and Owner of the school, she loves being involved every day with students, teachers, parents, and staff.

A career as a Montessori teacher gives her the opportunity to change tomorrow by working with today's children. She desires to help children reach their potential and guide them toward meaningful growth. In her free time, she enjoys hiking, exercising, reading, and spending time with her family and her dog Snoop.

Our Educational Philosophy

We believe that every child is born with an innate desire to learn and explore the world around them. Our role as educators is to create a prepared environment that honors this natural curiosity while providing the structure and guidance children need to thrive.

"The child is both a hope and a promise for mankind." - Dr. Maria Montessori

We embrace the Montessori principle that children learn best through hands-on exploration, meaningful work, and respectful relationships. Our mixed-age classrooms create a family-like atmosphere where children develop empathy, leadership skills, and a sense of community.

Every decision we make is guided by our commitment to helping children develop independence, confidence, and a lifelong love of learning. We celebrate each child's unique journey while preparing them for success in an ever-changing world.

Our Learning Environment

Prepared Classrooms

Carefully designed learning spaces with authentic Montessori materials that encourage exploration, independence, and concentrated work.

Outdoor Learning Garden

Natural outdoor spaces where children can explore nature, tend gardens, and engage in physical activities that support gross motor development.

Child-Sized Kitchen

Functional kitchen area where children can engage in food preparation activities, developing practical life skills and healthy eating habits.

Quiet Reading Corner

Cozy spaces designed for individual reading, reflection, and quiet activities that support language development and concentration.

Art & Creative Space

Dedicated areas for artistic expression, creative projects, and cultural activities that inspire imagination and self-expression.

Safe & Secure Environment

State-licensed facility with comprehensive safety measures, ensuring a secure environment where children can learn and grow with confidence.
